Nathaniel TeleMed

Nathaniel TeleMed is a 24-hour online specialist consultation service. Our virtual service is spread across Nigeria and diaspora.

Should you or any of your dependants need medical attention, you can call us anytime. Our doctor(s) will evaluate your complaints and give the necessary medical advice and treatment.

In some circumstances, physical examination is necessary to arrive at correct diagnosis and treatment. In Nigeria, in such situations, we advise the clients to consult a doctor for physical evaluation. We have many hospitals in our network where appropriate medical attention can be obtained. For clients in diaspora, we counsel them to access physical evaluation in their respective health system.

Depending on proximity to clients' home or office, if another hospital in our network is visited, an additional non-refundable fee for the service obtained at the respective facility would be paid by the client.
